About Me

When I first started working out my senior year of high school, I never thought that my fitness journey would take me this far. The growth I experienced along this path, will undoubtedly define how I approach life and my future endeavors, and I am indebted to that experience. I became addicted to my personal growth, so I decided to document it on Instagram, in all honesty, just for the fun of it. As I continued to post, one by one people began to take notice. Wether it was to motivate me or query about how to track macronutrients, I noticed that my viewers were benefiting and appreciating my content, which only pushed to continue to put my best foot forward.
